Selected Projects: Sandia Energy & Climate

This project was my baby for four years - the main website supporting the Energy & Climate program communications for Sandia National Laboratories. I was the solitary designer and developer, responsible for everything from updating infrastructure to design refreshes. This role gave me a great glimpse into how critical a holistic digital engagement strategy is for large organizations. Though the main purpose of the website is to be informational, it also provided a touchstone for outside organizations and other national labs to partner with Sandia on critical initiatives for the Department of Energy.

 

Contributions: UX, UI, Taxonomy, Usability, Development

Other Elements: site map, taxonomy strategy, sketches, wireframes, mockups, style guide.
